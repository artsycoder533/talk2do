Talk2Do

Talk2Do is a typing-free notes app that utilizes Speech Recognition


Directions: Press the micorphone button and say a command. Speak clearly and closely.


Supported Commands:

  • "Show instructions"
  • "Hide instructions"
  • "Add", Ex: "Add wash dishes"
  • "Edit", Ex: "Edit wash dishes"
  • "Delete", Ex: "Delete wash dishes"
  • "Delete all"
  • "Complete", Ex: "Complete wash dishes"
  • "Complete All"
  • "Undo", Ex: "Undo wash dishes"
  • "Undo all"
  • "Dark mode on"
  • "Dark mode off"
